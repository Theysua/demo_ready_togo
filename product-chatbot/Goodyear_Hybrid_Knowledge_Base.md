## COMPANY INTRODUCTION

### ABOUT GOODYEAR
Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company started in 1898 with just 13 workers. Today, with an annual revenue exceeding $20 billion, Goodyear has more than 72,000 employees and scientists working in different facilities across the world.

Cooper, Dunlop, Kelly, Fulda, Sava and Debica are the other tire brands produced by Goodyear.

### GOODYEAR'S LEADERSHIP
From the road to the racing track with record breaking performance, Goodyear continues to produce high quality tires to meet the stringent requirements of our OE customers and drivers across the world.

### GOODYEAR INNOVATION
Partnering with NASA for the moon tire to the first commercialize soybean oil-based tire, Goodyear is constantly innovating. We have received many international awards and recognitions for our continuous innovation and contribution towards mobility and the environment.

## Key Milestones

### 1898
Frank Seiberling founded The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company.

### 1964
Craig Breedlove set a speed record of 526.28 mph in a jet-powered Spirit of America vehicle equipped with Goodyear tires.

### 1983
Goodyear supplied radial tires for commercial aircraft approved by Federal Aviation Administration in North America.

### 1991
Goodyear introduced the Aquatred tire that delivers high performance traction in both wet and dry conditions.

### 1998
Goodyear became the first tire company to win 368 Formula 1 Grand Prix races.

### 2012
Goodyear's Air Maintenance Technology tire was recognized by Time Magazine as one of the best inventions of 2012.

### 2019
Goodyear returned to 24 Hours of Le Mans and FIA World Endurance Championship (WEC).

### 2021
Goodyear acquired the Cooper Tire & Rubber Company. The transaction expands Goodyear's market leadership in the U.S., nearly doubles the company's presence in China and strengthens its position in the global tire industry.

### 1907
1,200 sets of tires sold to Henry Ford for use on Model T automobile.

### 1971
First tires on the moon (Apollo 14) supplied by Goodyear.

### 1987
Goodyear unveiled the Spirit of Akron, the world's largest airship by volume and length during its time.

### 1992
Goodyear developed the first run-on-flat tire for the 1994 Chevrolet Corvette.

### 2009
Goodyear and NASA invented the "Spring Tire", an energy-efficient tire that won’t go flat.

### 2015
Goodyear introduced BH03 concept tire capable of charging the car battery.

### 2020
Goodyear developed an intelligent tire with built-in sensors.

### 2022
Goodyear joined Lockheed Martin to develop the next lunar vehicle tire.
